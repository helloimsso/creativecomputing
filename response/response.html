How has web design reflected out culture?

Global companies, such as Mc Donald’s shows different design styles to reach variety of people.
Designs are the most crucial communication tool for use in different cultures.
I assume that the most common tool is websites.
Over the last two decades, the number of Internet users has grown rapidly and the web
has become a significant and indispensable communication thing throughout the world. I think culture is
one of the most important phenomena that we can’t ignore in design and development of digital
products. Because these are reflected in users’ website preferences as well. Chinese, Japanese and Korean
users often expect to see a greater use of images, videos and sidebars, On the other hand,
Scandinavian or German designs tend to be more text-heavy. Actually, McDonald’s website is a perfect
example for cross-cultural website design. McDonald's websites show different countries' web styles and
designs according to their culture. In addition to text, it includes the type of image used, the
selected printing technique, color palette design, and selected layout.
Patterns of culture in the design can be identified as reflections of emotions, behaviors, and the
beliefs of users in different cultures.
